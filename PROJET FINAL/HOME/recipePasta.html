Bolonese Pasta

Next recipe >

Experience the essence of Italian cuisine in our Classic Bolognese Pasta recipe.

The ingredians

Pepper Pasta
Quantity : 1kg

Grape tomatoes
Quantity : 2

Olive oil
Quantity : 2 spoons

Beef Meat
Quantity : 450g

Follow the steps :

1

Cook the Vegetables: In a large skillet or saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Cook for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are softened.

2

Add the Ground Beef to the skillet. Break it apart with a spoon and cook until browned.

3

Stir in the minced garlic and tomato paste. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.

4

Add the crushed tomatoes and beef or vegetable broth to the skillet. Stir well.

5

Season the sauce with oregano, basil, thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. You can simmer longer for a richer flavor.

6

While the sauce is simmering, cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

7

Taste the Bolognese sauce and adjust the seasoning if needed. You can add a pinch of sugar to balance the acidity.

Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Pasta Bolognese!
